RISK MITIGATION & CONSTRAINTS
================================================================================

OPERATIONAL DISRUPTION:
  • Mitigation: Stagger migrations; test in parallel before cutover; maintain
    dedicated support desk for user questions during transition (30 days)

SECURITY & COMPLIANCE:
  • Mitigation: Require SOC 2 / ISO 27001 certification from consolidation
    targets; conduct data residency and encryption audits before migration

MISSION-CRITICAL SYSTEMS:
  • CRM platform: Requires zero-downtime migration and phased user rollout
    (5-day testing window before production cutover)
  • Travel/expense platform: Non-critical; can migrate in one weekend with
    72-hour rollback plan

VENDOR LOCK-IN & CONTRACTS:
  • Mitigation: Obtain legal review of early termination fees and contract
    flexibility; negotiate 30-day data access period post-consolidation

STAKEHOLDER RESISTANCE:
  • Mitigation: Engage sales leadership (CRM), finance team (advisory), and
    expense policy owners (travel) in consolidation planning 2 weeks prior
